Treasury

The treasury serves as support for The Growth Fund Wallet. Through Marketing and Product Development, Warp will provide a long-term sustainable yield solution for token holders.

Growth Fund

The WARP Growth Fund is a wallet which automatically buys back the $WARP token every 2 days. The wallet will be funded through a small portion of tax on each $WARP transaction.

The Wormhole

In order to sustain our rebase function, we implemented The Wormhole which is a dead wallet made to "burn" a part of Warp's supply and send tokens out of circulation.

NFT

If you lock $WARP you will receive a unique NFT which will represent the ownership and value of your tokens. On one hand claimable rebasing rewards will still be generated with locked Warp tokens and on the other hand the NFT creates a passive income from Treasury earnings.
